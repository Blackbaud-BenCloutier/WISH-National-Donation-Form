The Make-A-Wish Foundation grants the wishes of children with life-threatening medical conditions to enrich the human experience with hope, strength, and joy. It is the largest wish-granting charity in the world. With the help of generous donors and tens of thousands of volunteers, the Make-A-Wish Foundation has granted wishes to precious children since 1980. For more information on how you can share the power of a wish®, visit us at www.wish.org.
