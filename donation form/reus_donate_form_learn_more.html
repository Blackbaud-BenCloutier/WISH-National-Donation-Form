Benefits of membership

Close
  1. Great satisfaction. You will know you are helping wishes come true every month.
  2. Convenience. When you participate, your gift will be transferred and will appear each month on your credit card or checking account statement.
  3. Flexibility. If for any reason you wish to increase, decrease or suspend your gifts, just call (800) 722-9474, ext. 3320, and we will gladly accommodate you.
  4. Reduced paperwork and overhead expenses. Automatic monthly payments mean more of your donation goes to granting wishes.
  5. Make-A-Wish calendar. You will receive a year of inspiring photos to warm your heart.
  6. Periodic updates. You'll get ongoing new stories and information via mail or email.
