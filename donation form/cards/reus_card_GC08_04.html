Story on the back

I wish to go to the Walt Disney World® Resort Lisamarie, age 12

Lisamarie's wish came true when she visited the Walt Disney World® Resort. Lisamarie, who was diagnosed with Angelman syndrome and seizure disorder, loved whirling and twirling in the teacups on the Mad Tea Party ride, and her photo album is now filled with pictures of her posing with all of her Disney character friends.

About the Make-A-Wish Foundation®
The Make-A-Wish Foundation grants the wishes of children with life-threatening medical conditions to enrich the human experience with hope, strength and joy. It is the argest wish-granting charity in the world. With the help of generous donors and more than 25,000 volunteers, the Make-A-Wish Foundation has granted wishes to courageous children since 1980. For more information on how you can share the power of a wish®, visit us at www.wish.org.
